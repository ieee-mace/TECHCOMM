Techcomm

Techcomm is a 2-day workshop series organised by IEEE ComSoc SBC MACE in collaboration with the IEEE ComSoc Kerala Chapter, providing a platform for students to learn more about communication technology and improve their technical knowledge. Packed with fun, innovative, and informative sessions, Techcomm provides a new experience for its participants to learn and grow.
